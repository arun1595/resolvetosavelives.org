Our goal is to save 100M lives from cardiovascular disease and to prevent epidemics.

Resolve to Save Lives is a public health accelerator. We provide funding and operational support to governments and civil society in low- and middle-income countries, to implement proven strategies.

Recent work from our team

July 20, 2018

Cardiovascular Health team in Vietnam — Dr. Marc Jaffe and Meg Ferrell of the Resolve to Save Lives CVD team met with WHO colleagues and local health leaders to support the launch of Vietnam's hypertension control efforts.

July 16-21, 2018

Prevent Epidemics team members in Rwanda — Dr. Cyrus Shahpar and Le Quach, traveled to Rwanda to support the Ministry of Health. Dr. Shahpar presented the Resolve to Save Lives “step up” strategy to encourage countries to improve epidemic preparedness in four core areas.

July 2-6, 2018

Tech team pilots Simple app in India — The Resolve to Save Lives tech team, ran our first real-world pilot of the Simple Android application, which aims to help healthcare workers to longitudinally track their hypertensive patients.

June 22, 2018

PreventEpidemics.org Launch — Resolve to Save Lives launched PreventEpidemics.org, an interactive website which spotlights gaps in epidemic preparedness and highlights actions that countries, donors, activists and organizations can take to fill them.

May 30, 2018

Cardiovascular health team in China — Dr. Marc Jaffe traveled to China to meet with partners at the World Bank and others, and advance the hypertension prevention work. In this photo Dr. Jaffe is having his blood pressure measured by Professor Liu Lisheng of the Beijing Lisheng Health Foundation.

May 24, 2018

World Health Assembly Side Event — Dr. Tom Frieden participated in a panel discussion entitled “Reducing Sugar, Salt and Fat to Prevent NCDs.” Topics presented and discussed included the benefits of banning trans fat, Vital Strategies’ sugary drinks and sodium-reduction campaigns and case studies of industry efforts to block public health programs.

May 14, 2018

The Lancet publishes "REPLACE a roadmap to make the world trans fat free by 2023" written by Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus and Thomas R Frieden

May 12, 2018

WHO announces REPLACE — The World Health Organization announced REPLACE, a ground-breaking initiative developed in partnership with Resolve to Save Lives, which aims to eliminate trans fats from the global food supply by 2023. Read about it in The New York Times, CNN, The Wall Street Journal, or Vox.

May 1, 2018

Supporting Tanzania’s Preparedness Efforts — Dr. Cyrus Shahpar traveled to Tanzania to participate in the AFRO regional strengthening workshop for international health regulations. He was joined by colleagues from WHO AFRO Region, US HHS, Public Health England, WHO HQ, and AFRO staff.

April 4, 2018

Kerala launches IHMI — The state of Kerala launched the India Hypertension Management Initiative on Wednesday, April 4. Read about the launch in The Hindu.
